# What is SEO?

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. It is the process of improving a website so that it ranks higher in search engine results.

# Advantages of SEO

- Increases organic traffic from search engines.
- Improves website visibility and brand awareness.
- Builds trust and credibility with users.
- Provides long-term results compared to paid advertising.
- Targets users who are actively searching for relevant information.
- Helps improve user experience and site usability.
- Can lead to higher conversion rates.
